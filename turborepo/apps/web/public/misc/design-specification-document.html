Brand Philosophy

Your logo embodies the core principles of consilience - the unity of knowledge across disciplines. The interlocking "dd" represents the intersection of your biochemistry background with cutting-edge AI technology, creating something greater than the sum of its parts.

The overlapping circles suggest neural networks, molecular bonds, and impossible geometry - all unified in a clean, scalable mark that works across your entire platform ecosystem.

"The greatest challenge today, not just in cell biology and ecology but in all of science, is the accurate and complete description of complex systems."
— Edward O. Wilson

Your multi-model LLM platform is exactly this kind of complex system - bringing together diverse AI providers and models into a coherent, unified interface. The logo reflects this synthesis beautifully.
